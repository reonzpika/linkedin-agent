## Usage guidelines

**When to suggest a mention:**
- Post directly discusses that organisation's product, platform, or policy
- Post references a recent announcement or initiative from that entity
- Post builds on or responds to content they've published
- Post shares practice experience directly relevant to their domain

**When NOT to suggest a mention:**
- Generic topic discussion where entity is not central
- Hoping to get their attention for engagement
- Post is critical or negative (skip the tag, let them find it organically)
- Already tagging 2-3 other entities (hard limit)

**Verification note:**
URLs marked as "not verified" should be manually checked before first use. Most profiles are readily findable through LinkedIn's search.
